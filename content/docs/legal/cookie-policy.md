# Cookie Policy

**Effective Date:** March 1, 2026
**Last Updated:** March 1, 2026

This Cookie Policy ("Policy") explains how MuriData ("Company," "we," "us," or "our") uses cookies and similar tracking technologies when you visit the MuriData website and web application (collectively, the "Services").

This Policy should be read in conjunction with our [Privacy Policy](/docs/legal/privacy-policy).

## 1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device (computer, tablet, or mobile phone) when you visit a website. Cookies are widely used to make websites work more efficiently, provide a better user experience, and supply information to website operators.

Similar technologies include web beacons, pixels, local storage, and session storage, which serve comparable purposes. References to "cookies" in this Policy include these similar technologies.

## 2. Types of Cookies We Use

### 2.1. Strictly Necessary Cookies

These cookies are essential for the operation of the Services. They enable core functionality such as wallet connection state, page navigation, and access to secure areas. The Services cannot function properly without these cookies.

| Cookie | Purpose | Duration |
|--------|---------|----------|
| `wallet_connected` | Remembers wallet connection state | Session |
| `network_preference` | Stores preferred blockchain network | 30 days |

### 2.2. Functional Cookies

These cookies enable enhanced functionality and personalization, such as remembering your preferences and settings.

| Cookie | Purpose | Duration |
|--------|---------|----------|
| `theme_preference` | Stores light/dark mode preference | 1 year |
| `sidebar_collapsed` | Remembers documentation sidebar state | 30 days |
| `search_history` | Recent search queries for autocomplete | 7 days |

### 2.3. Analytics Cookies

These cookies help us understand how visitors interact with the Services by collecting and reporting information anonymously. We use this data to improve the Services.

| Cookie | Purpose | Duration |
|--------|---------|----------|
| Algolia Analytics | Search query analytics and relevance | Session |

### 2.4. Performance Cookies

These cookies collect information about how the Services perform, including page load times and error rates. This data is used to monitor and improve service performance.

## 3. Third-Party Cookies

The Services may include content or functionality provided by third parties, which may set their own cookies. These include:

3.1. **WalletConnect.** When using WalletConnect to connect your cryptocurrency wallet, WalletConnect may set cookies necessary for the wallet connection process. WalletConnect's use of cookies is governed by its own privacy policy.

3.2. **Algolia.** Our search functionality is powered by Algolia, which may set cookies for search analytics and performance optimization. Algolia's use of cookies is governed by its own privacy policy.

We do not control the cookies set by third parties and are not responsible for their cookie practices.

## 4. Cookie Management

### 4.1. Browser Settings

Most web browsers allow you to manage cookies through browser settings. You can typically:

- View cookies stored on your device
- Delete individual cookies or all cookies
- Block cookies from specific websites or all websites
- Set preferences for first-party and third-party cookies

Please note that blocking or deleting essential cookies may impair the functionality of the Services.

### 4.2. Browser-Specific Instructions

- **Chrome:** Settings > Privacy and Security > Cookies and other site data
- **Firefox:** Settings > Privacy & Security > Cookies and Site Data
- **Safari:** Preferences > Privacy > Manage Website Data
- **Edge:** Settings > Cookies and site permissions > Manage and delete cookies

### 4.3. Do Not Track

Some browsers offer a "Do Not Track" (DNT) feature. The Services do not currently respond to DNT signals, as no uniform standard for DNT has been adopted.

## 5. Local Storage and Session Storage

In addition to cookies, the Services use browser local storage and session storage to store certain data locally on your device. This includes:

5.1. **Wallet Connection Data.** Information necessary to maintain your wallet connection across page loads.

5.2. **Application State.** Temporary application state data required for the functioning of the web application.

5.3. **Cached Content.** Certain content may be cached locally to improve loading performance.

Local storage data persists until explicitly cleared by the user or the application. Session storage data is cleared when the browser tab is closed.

## 6. Consent

6.1. **Strictly Necessary Cookies.** These cookies do not require your consent as they are essential for the provision of the Services.

6.2. **Non-Essential Cookies.** For analytics, functional, and performance cookies, we rely on your consent. You may withdraw your consent at any time by adjusting your browser settings or contacting us.

6.3. **EEA/UK Users.** If you are located in the European Economic Area or the United Kingdom, non-essential cookies will only be placed on your device with your prior consent, in accordance with the ePrivacy Directive and applicable national laws.

## 7. Data Collected Through Cookies

Information collected through cookies may include:

- Pages visited and navigation patterns
- Time spent on pages
- Browser type and version
- Device type and screen resolution
- Referring website or source
- Search queries within the Services

This data is processed in accordance with our [Privacy Policy](/docs/legal/privacy-policy).
